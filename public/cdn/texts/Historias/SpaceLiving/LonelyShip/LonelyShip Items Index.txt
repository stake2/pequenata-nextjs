List of items that exist on every SpaceShip (LonelyShip)

1. LonelyShip Teleporter - LST, the Teleporter that teleports you between SpaceShips or to Earth
2. Thought Powered Fridge - TPF, the fridge that creates food and beverage for you
3. Very fast Supercomputer
4. Laser weapon outside of the spaceship to protect you from asteroids
5. Window to see the Earth, Moon and the Sun
6. Bathroom with a laser toilet bowl that melts everything that gets inside it
7. Chair for the computer
8. Wardrobe with your clothes from the Earth that were teleported to the SpaceShip
9. Bed to sleep
